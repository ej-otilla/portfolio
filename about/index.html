Hi, I'm Evan James Otilla.

You can call me Evan or EJ for short!

Originally from the Philippines, I've always been fascinated with technology and exploring the depths of my creativity.

Currently, I'm learning web design and development at the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology (SAIT), focusing on HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I'm particularly interested in user experience, visual design, and ensuring that everything I create is accessible to everyone, regardless of ability.

When I'm not working, I enjoy going out with friends, watching commentary and gaming videos on YouTube, and exploring all things music.

My Virtues

  1. User-Centric

    I focus on putting people at the heart of my designs. This approach guarantees that my work is intuitive, exciting, and, most importantly, accessible.

  2. Sustainable

    I place sustainability on quite a high pedestal. I make sure to have a positive impact on the environment by reducing waste and energy consumption, as well as using eco-friendly materials and creating long-lasting designs. Keeping our planet healthy is more important than ever, so I encourage you to make smart decisions.

  3. Story-Driven

    Every element in my designs is intentional and serves a meaningful purpose. I weave stories into my work to create a stronger connection and a more pleasing experience for users and clients.

  4. Dynamic

    My creations are always evolving, just like the rest of the world. I put a lot of effort into being adaptable and making sure my designs are engaging and responsive while remaining pertinent to the needs of my clients and users.
